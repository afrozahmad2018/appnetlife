Who We Are
Let us introduce ourself in a better way...
Founded in 1999, FusionSpark Media is a talent network of individuals with backgrounds in marketing, public relations, digital media and journalism.

We are strategists, communications professionals, photographers, filmmakers, writers and producers. Our offices are in Langley, WA (north of Seattle), Medford, NJ (east of Philadelphia), Florida and Nagoya, Japan.

Our roots run deep in the development of results-driven, purpose-focused non-fiction storytelling and marketing communications that inspires and informs people about products, services and causes and influences them to take actions.
